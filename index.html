Zhiwei Fang

Robotics PhD Student
EE, CUHK
I'm currently a second year PhD student in the department of electronic engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Hongliang Ren. My research interests are the neurosurgical robot and imaging, as well as the intelligent robotics. I have the experience of developing robot structures, optimized and learning-based control. My work also involves surgical perception utilization and surgical scenario applications.

Prior to that, I received the bachelor of science in mechanical engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), under the supervision of Prof. Shiwu Zhang and Prof. Wei Gao. My bachelor thesis title is 'Optimization of Locomotion Control for Two-Segmented Leg under Variable Payloads'.
